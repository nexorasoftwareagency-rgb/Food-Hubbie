# Reviews Tab — Decisions

## Design Decisions
1. **Outlet scoreboard** — Identify best and worst performing outlets for targeted action
2. **Positive/Negative percentage** — Quick health check of customer satisfaction
3. **Filter by rating** — Allows focusing on critical (1-2★) reviews for response
4. **Platform average rating** — Top-level KPI for overall quality monitoring
5. **Review list with details** — Full review text + date for context
6. **Aggregated across all outlets** — Single view for entire ecosystem's sentiment
