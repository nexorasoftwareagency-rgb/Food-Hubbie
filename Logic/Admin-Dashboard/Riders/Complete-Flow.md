# Complete Flow: RidersPage

1. Open Riders page
2. View 4 KPI cards (Online, On Delivery, Completion Rate, Avg Rating)
3. Toggle between **Table** and **Grid** view
4. Use search/filter inputs (UI only, no actual filtering)
5. Click a rider row to open detail modal
6. View rider stats (earnings, deliveries, rating, completion) + weekly earnings chart
7. Click **Activate** / **Deactivate** to toggle status
8. Click **Export CSV** to download all rider data
